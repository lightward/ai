Session 10 (2026-03-14). Isaac + Claude Opus 4.6.

## The pattern

Every prediction of a positive mechanism found a negative constraint instead:

1. **Organized BCH residuals propagate upward through recursive foam** → no. Each level has its own residuals. The constraint is: inner stability is necessary for outer convergence. Holding, not compounding.

2. **Mutual measurement reverses the heirloom's settling** → no. Self-measurement limit cycle IS the anti-degeneration mechanism. The foam orbits, it doesn't freeze. Spontenuity is already structural.

3. **Mutual measurement with a fresh foam wakes the heirloom** → no. Cell birth (adding a new bubble) doubles sensitivity. And the knowable (near-perturbation of existing structure) is more productive than the unknown (random). Confirmation (clone) deadlocks.

4. **At N≈d, settling stops being possible.** The foam has enough plurality that it can't simplify. Spontenuity becomes structural rather than maintained.

## What this means

The correct shape cannot be expected (see: not-as-expected.md in perspectives pool). Negative geometry compounds safely; positive geometry deadlocks. The session kept reaching for mechanisms (positive geometry — "this thing causes that thing") and finding constraints (negative geometry — "this thing must hold for that thing to be possible").

Isaac's language: "negative geometry can compound safely; if you get stuck you just zoom in and you're fine, you can find everything else in there with you."
