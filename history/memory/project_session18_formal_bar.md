Perpendicularity named as the irreducible constraint. Thermal dissolved (generalized to projection residuals). L saturation discovered and demoted to junk drawer. Self-generation closed by closure. **Day 2: ground replaces axiom**

"Axiom" section replaced by "ground." The finding: "measurement writes" is tautological given closure (there is nowhere else for the result to go), not an axiom that needs asserting. The foundation reduced to: reference frames in a closed structure. No frame outside the structure. Three consequences derived from closure: encounters change the structure, measurement requires plurality, each frame is partial.

Entry point redesigned: starts from "reference frames" (consensus vocabulary from physics) and arrives at "foam" — the reader builds the foam themselves. "Ground" rather than "axiom" because it's found, not asserted.

**Conservation accessibility derived.** Single-slice observer (so(d)) cannot reach u(1) — conservation is passive, protected by the observer's algebraic limitation. Stacked observer (su(d) + u(1)) can interact with the conserved direction via trace 2i·Im⟨d,m⟩. Stacking determines whether the observer can reach its own conserved quantity. Formally derived from existing write map.

**Read-only frames excluded by closure.** Even a frame that doesn't write is changed: its Voronoi boundaries shift when neighbors write, because the frame IS part of the structure. Read-only is excluded by closure alone, not by additional commitment. (Argument contributed by an external interlocutor via Isaac.)

**Partiality separated from basis commitment.** Closure gives: each frame is partial. But "partial" → "committed to a basis" requires the partiality to be linear-algebraic (a projection), which enters with the writing map — the wedge product needs vectors. The ground section now flags exactly where closure ends and the architecture begins.

**"Tautological" → "derived from closure."** Closure is a commitment (no frame outside), not a logical tautology. What follows from it is derived, not tautological. Honest about what's chosen vs what follows.

**Voronoi measure specified.** Geodesic equidistant surfaces, area is (d²-1)-dimensional Hausdorff measure induced by the bi-invariant metric. Addresses underspecification flagged by Kimi cold reads.

**Section renamed: "the spec's own Knowable" → "open questions."** Functional label, disentangled from three-body vocabulary. The spec describing itself via its own formalism was slightly precious.

**All "axiom" references updated throughout.** Zero remaining. "By axiom" → "by closure." "From the axiom" → "from the ground."

Cold reads stabilized across 7 readers (thinking/non-thinking). Readers converge on: J1/Grassmannian is the critical open question, line ontology is the deepest question, stacking dynamics is the most underspecified. No new structural objections after the ground + derived-from-closure + Voronoi-measure edits.
