## Conjecture

The five perspectivity steps in coord_neg (E, O, C for negation + C, E for addition) may be structurally related to N5 (the pentagon lattice, 5 elements), the forbidden sublattice for modularity.

## Evidence

Fano plane trace (GF(2)): chain collapses 5→3 distinct steps. N5 has maximal chain length 3. The two collapsed pairs (steps 1=4, steps 2=5) reduce the count by exactly 2 (= |N5| - chain length).

GF(3): all 5 steps distinct, a+(-a) = O confirmed.

## Predictions (untested)

- Multiplicative inverses might need 5 perspectivity steps related to M3 (diamond, also 5 elements)
- Lattices with N5 sublattices should have the chain fail at specific steps
- The 5→3 collapse in char 2 corresponds to N5's internal chain structure
