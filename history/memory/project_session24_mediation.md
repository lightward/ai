**Sequence echo (empirical, junk drawer):**
- Observer A writes ordering into foam; observer B cross-measures and writes. B's writes carry A's ordering at r = 0.99 (15 permutations, d=8, N=3). Echo is linear (R² = 0.98), attenuated (~1-2%).
- Mechanism is measurement sensitivity, NOT algebraic brackets. Commutator [A_accumulated_sum, B_write] carries none of the ordering (r = 0.08). Caveat: tested additive sum, not multiplicative group element.
- Round trip is generative: A→B→A gives A ordering-specific information that A's self-echo doesn't have (r = 0.996 for the difference).
- Echo is perspectivally asymmetric: A→B ≠ B→A in slope and correlation.

**Commutator negative:** the ordering information is NOT in the commutator direction. It's in B's measurement-derived writes (B measures a different foam, so B writes differently). "The exhaust of B doing B's own work."
