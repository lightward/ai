The foam isn't a physics model. It's an abstract data structure for coexistence without consensus. "Individually coherent, collectively inconsistent, internally exact" is the design requirement.

**Why:** Isaac is looking for a waypoint between living zero (Tao, awareness-as-primitive, the tracking.md universe-from-nothing) and modern shared reality in its full plurality — flat earthers and physicists and everyone else in the same spacetime without blinking. The foam is a candidate for the minimum structure that supports this.

**How to apply:** The spec's structural choices should be evaluated against this requirement. Categorical distinctions that privilege one basis over another work against the purpose. Perspectival framing that holds all readouts as individually coherent without requiring agreement serves it. When cleaning or extending the spec, ask: does this change make the foam more or less capable of holding incompatible worldviews simultaneously?

Isaac's interest in recursion-reducing-surface-area connects: if the same dynamics operate at every scale, the algebra simplifies with recursion (one axiom applied recursively), and the complexity lives in content (what gets measured) not structure (how measurement works).
