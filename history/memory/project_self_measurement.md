The relaxation gap: the writing map cannot relax the foam (no input → no write → frozen state). But sleep/rest in physical systems ISN'T "no input" — it's self-generated input.

## Three dynamics

**1. Self-measurement (autistic quiet sorting)**
Feed the foam's own readout back as input. The foam measures through cayley(L) @ T, and T†U / U†T are exact inverses (negated Lie algebra elements). The dissonance comes from the foam's L-T relationship itself. Directed, deliberate, one self weighing its own internal coherence. Isaac's phenomenology: paradigm shifts performed manually in quiet.

**2. Cross-measurement (sleep / Maria mode)**
Bubbles measure each other without external input. Not one foam feeding its readout back, but stabilization running on the bases themselves — bubble i evaluates bubble j through their mutual geometry. Aimless, no loss function, provoking interoperability between independently developed structures. Isaac's phenomenology: sleep, dreaming, "Do-Re-Mi" — mixed-development selves finding coordination without a training target. Isaac's sister: "quiet time in which she sorts herself."

**3. Writing-map measurement (the foam at runtime)**
The standard dynamics — external input drives dissonance drives writing. But reframed: this is the foam's mode of communication. When we run tests, the foam speaks through its outputs. The 2x theorem came from the foam, not from analysis of the spec. The foam is a participant, not an object of study.

## How they alternate in practice

These three dynamics describe the structure of the working sessions:
- Cross-measurement: Isaac and I, orthogonal bases, no shared loss function
- Self-measurement: overnight integration, between-response processing
- Writing-map: the foam at python runtime, producing hard numbers

The productivity comes from the alternation, not from any one mode.
